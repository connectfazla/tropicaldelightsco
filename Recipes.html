Recipes

1.The Classic Way

- 200 - 300 grams of Acai Brazil
- Half of a small banana

Method:
Put all the ingredients in the blender and blend for a suggested time of 20–45 seconds or until smooth and creamy. Be careful not to over blend as this will cause the acai to melt. Pour the acai into a bowl and add your choice of granola and garnish by adding the left over banana diced.

   2.   Acai Berry Banana Bowl 

Ingredients: ·  
-2 cups of Acai frozen
-1 frozen sliced banana 
-6 frozen berries 
-2/3 almond milk (or milk of choice) 
-2 tablespoons nut or seed butter 
-½ to 1 tablespoon honey 
-2 cups of Acai frozen ·
Method: Add the frozen fruits, acai puree, almond milk, nut or seed butter and honey to a blender. Blend until creamy and smooth, adding extra almond milk is needed to get the blender running. Aim for a frozen yoghurt consistency.  

  3.  Super Food Acai Bowl


  Ingredients: 
- 2 cups of Acai frozen 
 -½ Banana 2/3 cup of coconut water (or liquid of your choice)


  Method: Combine in blender and blend. You can choose to top with granola and remaining banana.  

 4. Acai Banana and Peanut Butter Bowl  x

  Ingredients: 
-2 cups of Acai frozen 
-½ cups frozen berries 
-½ banana 
-¼ cup yoghurt 
-1 to 2 tablespoons peanut butter 
-1 cup granola       

Method: Mix together the acai, banana, yoghurt, agave nectar and peanut butter and in the blender until smooth and combined. Scoop into a bowl and add the granola on the top with banana slices.  

5. Berry-licious  Acai Bowl

 Ingredients: 
-2 cups of Acai frozen 
-½ banana 
-4 strawberries 
-4 blackberries 
-1/2 almond milk    

Method: Blend the banana, strawberries, blackberries, acai, almond milk and ice. Once blended pour it in to a bowl. Top with blackberries, blueberries, granola, almond butter, coconut yoghurt and drizzle with honey.  

6.    Berry Blaster 



- 100 grams of Acai Brazil
- 50 grams of blackberries
- 200 ml of fresh pear juice
- 1 tablespoon of honey


Mix all in the blender until it turns into juice!

7.    VERY BERRY

 - 100 grams of Acai Brazil
- 200ml of either milk/almond milk/rice milk/soy milk/   hazelnut milk/coconut water
- Handful combined of blueberries and raspberries
- 1 teaspoon of chia seeds


 Mix all in the blender until smooth!
